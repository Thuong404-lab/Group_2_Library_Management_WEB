Library Rules

1. Present your student or library card when borrowing books.

2. The standard borrowing fee is VND 5,000 per book.

3. A reservation deposit of VND 50,000 per book is required.

4. The maximum loan period is 14 days, renewable for up to 7 additional days.

5. Overdue books incur a fine of VND 5,000 per day.

6. Your account will be locked after 3 overdue violations.

Severely damaged or lost books require the same compensation fee.
